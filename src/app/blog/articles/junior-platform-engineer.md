## Finding Platform Engineering
Throughout my years in University, I was always trying out new technologies and new tools to see what "niche" would grab ahold of my attention. 
I wanted to know early on which domain would ultimately become my home, but I had no idea of **what** domain of software engineering would even best suit me.
Would it be frontend? I did like working on Vue.js when I was on the frontend team at Nextcloud.
Would it be data engineering? I did some data engineer work when I was at Enova, but not enough to interest me, and I also found that working on services that other developer would
end up using turned out to be more fun anyways.
